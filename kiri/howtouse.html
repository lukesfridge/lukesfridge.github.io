How to Use Kiri Text Rendering Engine

Step 1

Click "See Inside"

Step 2

Drag the "Kiri-1.0.0" sprite into your backpack. If you want to use Sniper Mode, drag the "SniperMode-KiriExt" sprite into your backpack aswell.

Step 3

In your new project, drag the "Kiri-1.0.0" sprite into your sprite list. And "SniperMode-KiriExt" if you need Sniper Mode.

Step 4

Remove this section of code. It's only needed for the demo.

You may want to disable the options menu to stop users from accidentally messing up your project! You can do this in the options menu.

You're done!

Now, if you want to render some text, use the newTxt block. Use broadcasts.
